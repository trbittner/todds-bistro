01 New Brunch Recipe
2 15 min Beginner 248
These last few weeks I have been working hard on a new brunch recipe for you all.
Ingredients
  • 3 cups berries.
  • 1/2 lb. manchego cheese
  • 6 Hard boiled eggs
Instructions

1. Find a bowl. 2. Slice manchego into 1/4 inch slices. 3. Slice hard boiled eggs in half. 4. Layer eggs in the bowl. 5. Arrange cheese around the bowl's perimeter. 6. Top with berries and serve.
